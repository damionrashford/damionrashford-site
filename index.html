about

Building the tooling layer for AI agents.

I work where language models meet real systems — the Model Context Protocol, agent orchestration, retrieval, and the deterministic plumbing that turns an agentic demo into something you can run in production.

Most of my work is open source, and the point is that other developers actually pull it into their stacks. I care about auditable output, clean interfaces, and tools that don't fall over the first time real data hits them.

Right now I'm focused on the infrastructure that makes agents dependable — the servers, the scoring, the routing — and I'm open to roles where that's the job.
