About

On the work

I build the tooling layer that makes AI agents dependable — the servers, the retrieval, the scoring, the routing. I'm an investigator by temperament: gathering and evaluating information is the thing I'm best at, and most of what I ship is in service of doing that well, deterministically, at scale.

By day I work at Shopify, deep in the GraphQL Admin and Storefront APIs, developer tooling, and production debugging — tracing real issues across logs and live systems for the developers building on the platform. By night I build the open-source MCP servers and agents catalogued above.

The through-line is auditable over impressive. I'd rather ship a tool that returns the same answer twice and shows its work than one that demos well and falls over on real data. Stars are honest feedback; production use is better.

"Deterministic over clever."— operating principle
